# APEROL SPRITZ

## RECEPIE
- 75 ml prosseco
- 75 ml aperol
- 25 ml club soda 
- orange slices
- ice cubes

## HOW TO PREPARE 

1. Fill your **large wine glass** with plenty of **ice**.
2. Add 3 parts **Prosecco**, then 2 parts **Aperol**.
3. Add a dash of **club soda** and stir briefly.
4.  Garnish with an **orange slice** and enjoy!
